Our Story

Welcome to Krishika Foods and Pickles. I am a 50-year-old entrepreneur from Nellore, Andhra Pradesh, and my daughter, Krishika, is the inspiration behind this venture. We specialize in bringing you the authentic taste of homemade foods and pickles, both vegetarian and non-vegetarian, with the love and care of home-cooked meals.

Our Mission

To deliver fresh, homemade pickles and food to your doorstep, ensuring the best quality, taste, and traditional flavors. We strive to bring the taste of Andhra to every home, maintaining authenticity and excellence.
