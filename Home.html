Founder...!

Namaste! I’m Krishika, a passionate entrepreneur and a proud mother. The journey of creating "Krishika Pickles" started from a place of love for traditional recipes and family. As a mother, life was a whirlwind of responsibilities—working, managing the household, and raising my children. Amidst the chaos, I found myself missing the comforting flavors of homemade meals, especially the pickles my mother used to prepare. One day, I thought—why not bring those authentic, homemade flavors to others who might feel the same way? That’s when "Krishika Pickles" was born. I decided to step away from my career and fully dedicate myself to crafting pickles with the same care and tradition I grew up with. Soon enough, our pickles gained popularity, encouraging me to focus entirely on this venture. Now, every jar of "Krishika Pickles" is made with love, ensuring that each bite carries the warmth of home-cooked tradition. Join us, and experience the taste of love and tradition, passed down from my kitchen to yours.
